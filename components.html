Components

Special markup for various components that you can use in your site.

Overview

Special markup for various components that you can use in your site that are not normally included in Bootstrap.

Hero

Hero units are made to callout specific content. They are very similar to content call-outs.

A top university designed for you

Become a master learner—one who is able to thrive in tomorrow's dynamic work environment—at one of the world's most innovative universities today.

<div class="hero">
  ...
</div>

Image Heros

Hero units can also have background images.

A top university designed for you

Become a master learner—one who is able to thrive in tomorrow's dynamic work environment—at one of the world's most innovative universities today.

<div class="hero hero-bg-img hero-action-call">
  ...
</div>

Additional CSS can be provided to change the background image 

.hero-bg-img.hero-action-call {
  background-image: url(../img/hero-background-1.jpg);
  filter: progid:DXImageTransform.Microsoft.AlphaImageLoader(src='../img/hero-background-1.jpg', sizingMethod='scale');
  -ms-filter: "progid:DXImageTransform.Microsoft.AlphaImageLoader(src='../img/hero-background-1.jpg', sizingMethod='scale')";
}

Content call-outs

Content Call outs are darker versions of hero units. These should not be placed near the top of the navigation since they are similar colors in the default theme.

A top university designed for you

Become a master learner—one who is able to thrive in tomorrow's dynamic work environment—at one of the world's most innovative universities today.

<div class="hero hero-text-only hero-text-only-dark">
  ...
</div>
