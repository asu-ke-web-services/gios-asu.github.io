Welcome to the School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences

Our mission is to create and disseminate research and to provide outstanding instruction at the undergraduate and graduate levels in mathematics and statistics. Our distinguished faculty is involved in research in many areas within applied mathematics, theoretical mathematics, mathematics education, and statistics.
